Broadcasting your Pioneer DDJ-SX2 mix on a Mac

Selecting the SX2 as the Audio Device in Butt does not work normally, but you can make it work if you follow these steps. Note that any microphone plugged into the SX2 will not output to the broadcast stream.

You will need the latest versions of:

Butt
Soundflower
LineIn

Once they are installed, start Serato and play something.

Open Audio Midi Setup from your Applications/Utilities folder.

You should see a Device called PIONEER DDJ-SX2 2IN2OUT

Click the + button and create a new Aggregate Device.

Change the name of the new Device by clicking on its name, and enter  PIONEER DDJ-SX2 2IN2OUT. It is important that the name is exactly the same as the original device. The devices get renamed eventually, so they end up looking like this:

Then start LineIn and select Input from Soundflower (2 ch), Output to PIONEER DDJ-SX2 2IN2OUT. Then click Play Thru.

 
Start Butt and in the Audio tab of Settings select Soundflower (2ch) as the Audio Device.

You should hear audio through your amp and see the levels in Butt showing your broadcast volume.

Aim to keep it in the Green or it will clip.

Note that the Master Out and Booth Out controls on the SX have no effect on the broadcast volume, which means the level of each deck is very important - keep those in the green too!
